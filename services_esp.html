Silver

Jalisco is the main producer of silver in the country. The preference for this metal grew at a rate that exceeded gold sales. The workshops converted their production to make silver.

Zinc

World zinc production during 2019 reached a total of 12.40 million metric tons. The main producing country is China, followed by Peru and Australia.

Lead

A study by Wood Mackenzie forecasts that global lead production, excluding that of China, will increase in 2019 by about 250 kt, which is equivalent to over 10% of current output.
